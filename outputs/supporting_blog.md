Title: What Is SEO and Why Is It Important for Small Businesses?
Introduction
Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website's visibility on search engines like Google. SEO helps businesses attract organic traffic and increase online presence.
Key Points
Improves website ranking on search engines.
Increases organic traffic.
Builds brand credibility and trust.
Generates quality leads.
Provides long-term business growth.
Conclusion
Investing in SEO helps small businesses compete effectively in the digital marketplace and achieve sustainable growth.
