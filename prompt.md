# Prompt.md

## Project Title
AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator for a Digital Marketing Agency

---

## Objective

Create an SEO-optimized content strategy for a Digital Marketing Agency by generating a pillar blog, supporting blogs, keyword strategy, and internal linking structure to improve search engine rankings and increase organic traffic.

---

## Prompt Used

### Main Prompt

"Act as an SEO Content Strategist for a Digital Marketing Agency. Generate a complete SEO content cluster strategy around the primary keyword 'Digital Marketing Services'. Include:

1. A detailed keyword strategy containing:
   - Primary keywords
   - Secondary keywords
   - Long-tail keywords
   - Local SEO keywords

2. A comprehensive pillar blog titled 'Digital Marketing Services: The Complete Guide for Small Businesses in 2026'.

3. Four supporting blog topics with unique content.

4. An internal linking strategy connecting all supporting blogs with the pillar page.

5. SEO best practices to improve website rankings and organic traffic.

The content should be SEO-friendly, informative, easy to read, and targeted toward small business owners."

---

## Supporting Prompts

### Prompt 1: Keyword Strategy

"Generate an SEO keyword strategy for a Digital Marketing Agency. Include primary, secondary, long-tail, and local SEO keywords."

---

### Prompt 2: Pillar Blog

"Write a detailed pillar blog on 'Digital Marketing Services: The Complete Guide for Small Businesses in 2026' with proper H1, H2, and H3 headings."

---

### Prompt 3: Supporting Blogs

"Generate supporting blog articles related to Digital Marketing Services covering SEO, Social Media Marketing, PPC Advertising, and Content Marketing."

---

### Prompt 4: Internal Linking Strategy

"Create an internal linking strategy for a Digital Marketing Agency content cluster that improves SEO performance and user navigation."

---

## Expected Output

- SEO keyword list
- Pillar blog article
- Four supporting blogs
- Internal linking structure
- SEO recommendations

---

## Tools Used

- ChatGPT
- Google Keyword Planner
- SEMrush
- Google Search Console

---

## Outcome

A complete SEO content cluster was created for a Digital Marketing Agency to improve search visibility, increase website traffic, and generate business leads.
